# Parramatta Central: The Pinnacle of Western Sydney Living

## Project Overview
Parramatta Central is a landmark residential development located in the heart of Sydney's second CBD. Designed by world-renowned architects, this 55-story tower offers unparalleled views across the Sydney basin and towards the Blue Mountains.
