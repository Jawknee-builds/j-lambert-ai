# Southbank Residences: Melbourne's Arts District Luxury

## Project Overview
Located at 80 City Rd, Southbank Residences places you at the epicenter of Melbourne's cultural and entertainment precinct. This boutique collection of residences is designed for the discerning urbanite who values art, culture, and convenience.

## Available Residences
- **Studios & 1-Beds:** From $550,000 (High rental yield potential)
- **2-Bedroom Corner Suites:** From $950,000 (Features floor-to-ceiling glass and city views)
- **3-Bedroom Sky Villas:** From $1,800,000 (Double-height ceilings and custom marble kitchens)

## Exclusive Amenities
- **Private Cinema:** Bookable via the resident app for private screenings.
- **Climate-Controlled Wine Cellar:** Individual lockers for each resident.
- **The Arts Lounge:** A curated space featuring local Australian artworks.
- **Rooftop Wellness Deck:** Yoga studio, sauna, and heated outdoor lounge.
